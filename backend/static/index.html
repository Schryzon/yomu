yōmu! (ヨォム)

Reading Japanese,
made effortless.

yōmu! is an accessibility and learning augmentation layer. It eliminates the friction of switching tabs to dictionaries by automatically injecting furigana directly into the webpage, helping you natively read Japanese content without breaking your flow.

How it Works

1

Install

Add the yōmu! extension to your browser or use the web demo.

2

Scan

Navigate to any site. yōmu! automatically detects supported languages.

3

Read

Click the widget to see pronunciation guides and deep grammar analysis.

On the Go?

You can use yōmu! on your mobile devices too:

  • iOS: Install our Safari Extension to get furigana on any webpage.
  • Multi-Script: Instant Latin transliteration for Arabic, Russian, and Hindi.
  • Web Demo: Save this page to your home screen for quick access.
